Shea Butter

Extracted from the nuts of the African shea tree, shea butter is incredibly moisturizing and packed with vitamins and fatty acids.

Benefits of Shea Butter

Shea butter is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and ability to soothe dry, irritated skin.

It adds a luxurious, creamy texture to our soaps and helps maintain skin's natural moisture balance.
