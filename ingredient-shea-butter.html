Shea Butter

Extracted from the nuts of the African shea tree, shea butter is incredibly moisturizing and packed with vitamins and fatty acids.

Benefits of Shea Butter

  • Deeply Moisturizing: Rich in fatty acids and vitamins A, E, and F, shea butter provides intense hydration and helps restore the skin's natural moisture barrier.
  • Anti-Inflammatory Properties: Contains cinnamic acid and other natural compounds that calm inflammation and soothe irritated or sensitive skin conditions such as eczema and psoriasis.
  • Promotes Skin Healing: Encourages cell regeneration, helping to fade scars, stretch marks, and blemishes over time.
  • Anti-Aging Benefits: Boosts collagen production and neutralizes free radicals, helping to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Natural Sun Protection: Offers a low level of UV protection (SPF 3–4) while also soothing sun-damaged skin.
  • Softens and Conditions: Creates a protective layer on the skin that locks in moisture, leaving skin feeling soft, smooth, and supple.
  • Non-Comedogenic: Absorbs readily into the skin without clogging pores, making it suitable for all skin types including oily and acne-prone skin.
  • Luxurious Soap Texture: Adds a creamy, conditioning quality to handmade soaps, enhancing the overall bathing experience.
