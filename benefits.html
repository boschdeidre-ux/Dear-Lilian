Benefits of Our Ingredients

At Dear Lilian, we carefully select each ingredient for its natural benefits and skin-loving properties. Our transparent ingredient lists mean you know exactly what you're putting on your skin.

Tallow

Our main and most natural ingredient. Tallow from grass-fed animals is remarkably beneficial for your skin.

Learn More

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is naturally antibacterial and antimicrobial, helping to cleanse and protect the skin.

Learn More

Castor Oil

Castor oil is known for its exceptional moisturizing and healing properties.

Learn More

Olive Oil

Known for its moisturizing and antioxidant properties, olive oil is rich in vitamins E and A.

Learn More

Shea Butter

Extracted from the nuts of the African shea tree, shea butter is incredibly moisturizing and packed with vitamins and fatty acids.

Learn More

Essential Oils

We use only pure essential oils—no synthetic scents. Each oil is chosen for its unique skin benefits, from lavender's soothing properties to tea tree's antibacterial power.

Learn More

Flax Gel

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants, flax gel is a natural humectant that deeply hydrates, soothes irritation, and improves skin elasticity.

Learn More

Cold-Process Method

Our cold-process method preserves the natural benefits of these ingredients. Unlike mass-produced soaps that use high heat (which can degrade nutrients), cold processing maintains the integrity of vitamins, minerals, and beneficial compounds. This traditional method also produces natural glycerin, a humectant that attracts moisture to the skin—something that's often removed in commercial soap production.
