About Dear Lilian

Our Story

Dear Lilian was born from a passion for natural, sustainable living and a love for traditional soap-making. Based in Johannesburg, we craft each bar by hand using time-honored cold-process methods that preserve the natural goodness of our ingredients.

Our name pays homage to Lilian, a grandmother who believed in the simple beauty of handmade goods and the importance of treating both our bodies and our planet with care and respect.

Our Values

Natural Ingredients

We use only natural, skin-loving ingredients like olive oil, coconut oil, shea butter, and pure essential oils. No synthetic fragrances, harsh chemicals, or unnecessary additives.

Sustainable Practices

From our recycled paper packaging to our commitment to minimal waste, we're dedicated to reducing our environmental footprint. We offer refill options and encourage customers to recycle.

Small Batch Quality

Every batch is made with care and attention to detail. Our cold-process method takes longer, but it produces a superior soap that's gentle on skin and full of natural glycerin.

Community First

We believe in supporting local suppliers and giving back to our Johannesburg community. A portion of our profits goes to local environmental initiatives.

Join Our Journey

We're constantly learning and growing. Thank you for supporting small-batch, sustainable soap making.
