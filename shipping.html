Shipping & Returns

Delivery Information

Where we deliver

We currently deliver to all major centres across South Africa via courier. Orders within Johannesburg and Pretoria may qualify for local drop-off — please contact us to arrange this.

Processing time

Because everything is handmade in small batches, please allow 2–3 business days for your order to be prepared and packaged. Items marked "made to order" (such as the Natural Face Serum) may take up to 5 business days.

Delivery time

  • Gauteng: 1–2 business days after dispatch
  • Other major centres (Cape Town, Durban, Port Elizabeth): 2–3 business days
  • Remote areas: 4–7 business days

Shipping costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. We strive to keep shipping affordable — for orders over ZAR350 we offer free standard shipping to all main centres.

Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched you will receive a tracking number via email. If you have not received a tracking number within 3 business days of placing your order, please get in touch.

Returns & Refunds

Our commitment

We want you to love your Dear Lilian products. If something is not right, we will do everything we can to make it right.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the item and your order details. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Change of mind

Due to the handmade and personal care nature of our products, we are unable to accept returns simply because you changed your mind, unless the item is unopened and in its original packaging. Please contact us within 14 days of receiving your order to discuss this.

Made-to-order items

Items made to order (e.g. the Natural Face Serum) are non-refundable unless they arrive damaged or defective, as they are prepared specifically for you.

How to request a return

Email us at bosch.deidre@gmail.com with your order number, a description of the issue, and photos where applicable. We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is tallow soap?

Tallow is rendered beef fat that has been used in soap-making for centuries. It is remarkably similar in composition to the fats naturally found in human skin, making it deeply moisturising and non-irritating — even for sensitive skin types.

Is your soap cruelty-free?

Our products are never tested on animals. Our tallow is sourced as a by-product of the meat industry, and we are committed to using every part of an animal responsibly and with minimal waste.

How long does a bar last?

With proper care — keeping the soap dry between uses and storing it on a well-drained soap dish — a 100g bar typically lasts 4–6 weeks with daily use.

Are your soaps suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our soaps are free from synthetic fragrances and harsh chemicals. The unfragranced tallow soap is especially popular with those who have eczema, psoriasis, or other skin sensitivities. If you are unsure, please contact us for a recommendation.

Can I place a custom or bulk order?

Absolutely! We welcome custom orders for gifts, events, or wholesale inquiries. Please get in touch with your requirements and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Do you ship outside South Africa?

International shipping is not currently available, but we are working on it. If you are based outside South Africa and would like to order, please contact us and we will explore options together.

Still have a question?

We are happy to help. Reach out via our contact form or WhatsApp and we will get back to you within 1–2 business days.
