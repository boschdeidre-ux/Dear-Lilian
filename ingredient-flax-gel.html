Flax Gel

Derived from flaxseeds soaked in water, flax gel is a nutrient-rich, plant-based ingredient celebrated for its remarkable skin-softening and soothing properties, making it a wonderful addition to natural skincare.

Benefits of Flax Gel

  • Intense Hydration: Flax gel acts as a natural humectant, drawing moisture into the skin and locking it in for long-lasting hydration and softness.
  • Rich in Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Packed with alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), flax gel nourishes the skin barrier, helping to restore suppleness and a healthy glow.
  • Soothes Irritated Skin: Its natural anti-inflammatory compounds calm redness, itching, and irritation, making it ideal for sensitive or reactive skin types.
  • Improves Skin Elasticity: Regular use helps boost collagen support and skin elasticity, contributing to a firmer, more youthful appearance.
  • Antioxidant Protection: Flax gel is rich in lignans and vitamin E, powerful antioxidants that protect the skin from environmental stressors and free radical damage.
  • Balances Skin Texture: Helps smooth rough or uneven skin texture, leaving the skin feeling velvety soft and looking refreshed.
  • Gentle on Sensitive Skin: Free from harsh chemicals and synthetic additives, flax gel is gentle enough for even the most delicate skin, including eczema-prone skin.
  • Promotes Skin Healing: The natural mucilage in flax gel forms a protective layer over the skin, supporting the healing of minor irritations and maintaining the skin's natural moisture balance.
  • Lightweight and Non-Greasy: Unlike heavier oils, flax gel absorbs quickly into the skin without leaving a greasy residue, making it suitable for all skin types including oily and combination skin.
