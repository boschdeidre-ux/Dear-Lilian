Essential Oils

We use only pure essential oils for fragrance—no synthetic scents.

Benefits of Essential Oils on Your Skin

Pure essential oils go beyond fragrance—each one brings its own set of skin-loving properties that work in harmony with our other natural ingredients. Here are the essential oils we use and what they do for your skin:

Lavender Essential Oil

  • Soothes Irritation: Calms redness, inflammation, and irritated skin, making it ideal for sensitive skin types.
  • Promotes Healing: Supports the skin's natural repair process, helping minor blemishes and dry patches heal faster.
  • Antibacterial Action: Natural antibacterial properties help keep pores clean and reduce breakouts.
  • Relaxing Aromatherapy: Its calming scent reduces stress, which can have a positive knock-on effect on skin health.

Tea Tree Essential Oil

  • Powerful Antibacterial: One of nature's strongest antibacterial agents, keeping skin clean and free from harmful bacteria.
  • Antifungal Properties: Helps combat fungal skin concerns naturally.
  • Controls Excess Oil: Helps balance sebum production, making it great for oily and acne-prone skin.
  • Reduces Blemishes: Targets breakouts and helps prevent new ones from forming.

Peppermint Essential Oil

  • Cooling Sensation: Provides an invigorating, cooling effect on the skin, ideal for refreshing tired or overheated skin.
  • Antimicrobial: Natural antimicrobial properties help keep skin clean and healthy.
  • Soothes Itchiness: The menthol in peppermint oil calms itchy or irritated skin.
  • Brightening Effect: Helps improve skin radiance and gives a healthy glow.

Frankincense Essential Oil

  • Anti-Aging: Promotes cell regeneration and helps reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Skin-Toning: Helps tighten and tone the skin for a firmer, more youthful look.
  • Reduces Scarring: Supports the fading of scars, stretch marks, and dark spots over time.
  • Anti-Inflammatory: Calms redness and inflammation for an even, balanced complexion.

Eucalyptus Essential Oil

  • Deep Cleansing: Helps remove dirt and excess oil from pores for a thoroughly clean feel.
  • Antibacterial & Antifungal: Naturally fights bacteria and fungi on the skin's surface.
  • Refreshing & Invigorating: Uplifting scent energizes the senses while its properties revive dull skin.
  • Wound Healing: Supports the skin's natural healing response for minor cuts and irritation.

Lemon Essential Oil

  • Brightening: Rich in vitamin C, it helps brighten the complexion and even out skin tone.
  • Astringent: Helps tighten pores and reduce excess oiliness.
  • Antioxidant Protection: Protects the skin from environmental damage caused by free radicals.
  • Clarifying: Naturally clarifies and refreshes skin for a clean, healthy appearance.
