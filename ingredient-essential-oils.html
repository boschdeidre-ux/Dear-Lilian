Benefits of Essential Oils

Essential oils provide natural aromatherapy benefits while offering various skin benefits.

For example, lavender is calming and helps soothe irritation, while tea tree has natural antibacterial properties. Each essential oil is carefully selected to complement the soap's purpose and provide a gentle, natural fragrance.
